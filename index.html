Every session is intuitively guided and tailored to you. We begin with a consultation and intention setting, move into energy clearing and healing, and close with gentle integration. You stay comfortable and relaxed throughout.

Yes. Sessions are available both in person in Dublin and online, with the same depth and care either way.
